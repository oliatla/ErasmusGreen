📌 About these worksheets

These worksheets are part of GREEN Erasmus+ (KA220-VET), Work Package 2: Creation of teaching materials on efficient use of energy and water. Five partner schools each lead one topic area, creating materials in all partner languages.

Difficulty levels are designed so teachers can choose appropriate worksheets for their students — from hands-on observation (Basic) through calculation exercises (Medium) to full analysis and recommendation projects (Advanced).
